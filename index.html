I find inspiration in nature, people, architecture and mechanical designs. I love to take pictures of interesting vehicles, animals and people. I do not focus on one subject matter, and love to experiment and learn new tricks and techniques. I have worked as a professional studio photographer at SOOTER's Studios in Ottawa.
